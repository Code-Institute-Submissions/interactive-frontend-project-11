Manila

Manila, the capital of the Philippines, is a densely populated bayside city on the island of Luzon, which mixes Spanish colonial architecture with modern skyscrapers. Intramuros, a walled city in colonial times, is the heart of Old Manila. Noteable Places

Pin A: Sofitel Philippine Plaza Manila

During your stay at Sofitel you may never want to leave. This high end resort offers bay views, a lagoon style pool. As well as an award winning buffet at Sofitel's Spiral restaurant. Right next door is the Seascape Village where you have your choice of fresh seafood, cooked just the way you want it.

Pin B: SM Mall of Asia

SM Mall of Asia or MoA for short, is on the list for one of the world's largest malls. Situated by the scenic Manila Bay, it has world-class shops, restaurants and entertainment. Go to MoA for one day and it'll probably become two or three.

Pin C: Manila Ocean Park

This is the first world-class marine theme park in the Philippines. It has an array of attractions, views of the bay, enriching marine life exhibits, entertaining, dining and even a integrated resort. All this in one marine-themed package.
